Who are we?

STAM is the world’s first AI speech therapy service! Targeted towards children with articulation disorders, we hope to use fun gamified learning and real-time feedback to help children improve their speech. Our mission is to ensure all children, regardless of financial status, speech therapy wait times, and learning disabilities have access to the speech therapy they need!
